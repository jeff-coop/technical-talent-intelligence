# Companies

This page tracks companies that consistently develop, attract, or produce strong technical talent.

The goal is not to rank companies.

Instead, I'm interested in understanding which organizations create environments where engineers can develop technical depth, ownership, leadership, and domain expertise.

Many of these companies are also known for strong engineering cultures, technical challenges, open-source contributions, and influential engineering blogs.

## What I Look For Beyond Company Name

A company name alone does not determine candidate quality.

I generally care more about:

* Technical depth
* Ownership
* Impact
* Curiosity
* Learning velocity
* Leadership
* Communication
* Problem-solving ability

Strong candidates can come from anywhere.

The goal is to understand the environments and experiences that help exceptional talent develop.
