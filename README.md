# Technical Talent Intelligence

Hey, I'm Jeff 👋

I'm a Technical Recruiter based in New York focused on software engineering, AI, data, infrastructure, security, and technical talent intelligence.

Over the last several years I've recruited across startups, fintech, enterprise software, and large-scale engineering organizations.

Along the way I found myself constantly researching:

- Engineering teams
- Open-source communities
- University programs
- Research labs
- Internships
- Technical concepts
- Engineering ecosystems

At some point I had spreadsheets, bookmarks, saved articles, screenshots, and random notes scattered everywhere.

This repository is my attempt to organize that research and share it publicly.

The goal is simple:

> Better understand where exceptional technical talent comes from, how it develops, and where it goes.

## Why This Exists

Most recruiting resources focus on tools, sourcing techniques, and Boolean strings.

Those things are important.

What interests me most is understanding the ecosystem behind the talent.

Questions like:

- Why do certain universities consistently produce exceptional engineers?
- Which internships create strong technical foundations?
- Which open-source communities develop great engineers?
- What experiences tend to show up repeatedly among top performers?
- How do engineering talent markets evolve over time?

This repository is my attempt to answer those questions.
