# Open Source

This page tracks open-source projects, communities, and ecosystems that consistently attract and develop strong technical talent.

Open source is one of the most visible ways engineers can learn, collaborate, and contribute outside of their day-to-day jobs.

While not every strong engineer contributes to open source, it can provide valuable signals around technical depth, curiosity, ownership, and community involvement.

## Why Open Source Matters

Open-source projects often provide opportunities to:

* Learn from experienced engineers
* Work on real-world systems
* Contribute to widely used software
* Collaborate across organizations
* Develop technical credibility
