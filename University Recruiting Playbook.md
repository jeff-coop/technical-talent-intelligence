# University Recruiting Playbook

This page tracks universities, research programs, student organizations, competitions, and communities that consistently produce strong technical talent.

The goal is not to rank schools.

The goal is to better understand the ecosystems that help students develop into exceptional engineers, researchers, founders, and technical leaders.

# What Matters Most

School name alone is rarely enough.

I generally care more about:

* Curiosity
* Technical depth
* Ownership
* Research
* Open source contributions
* Learning velocity
* Leadership

Exceptional talent can come from any university.

The goal is to understand the experiences that help people grow.
