# Engineering Career Paths

This page explores common career paths across engineering, product, AI, infrastructure, research, and technical leadership.

The goal is not to define a single path.

Most successful technical careers are non-linear.

Instead, I'm interested in understanding the experiences, environments, and opportunities that help people develop throughout their careers.

---

# Observations

Some recurring themes I continue to see:

* Ownership compounds over time.
* Curiosity often predicts growth.
* Strong mentors can accelerate careers.
* Technical depth creates leverage.
* Exceptional careers are rarely linear.
* Learning velocity matters at every stage.
