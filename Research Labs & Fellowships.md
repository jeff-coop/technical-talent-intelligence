# Why I Track These Programs

Many exceptional candidates will never appear in a traditional sourcing search.

Research labs, fellowships, and residency programs often surface:

* Future founders
* Future staff engineers
* Future researchers
* Future technical leaders

Understanding these ecosystems helps identify talent earlier in their careers.
