# Infrastructure Engineering Deep Dive

This page is my attempt to better understand the infrastructure engineering ecosystem, the problems infrastructure engineers solve, and the technologies they work with.

The goal is not to become an infrastructure engineer.

The goal is to better understand the environments, challenges, and technical concepts that shape exceptional infrastructure talent.

---

# What Is Infrastructure Engineering?

Infrastructure engineering focuses on building and operating the systems that enable software to run reliably at scale.

While product engineers build customer-facing functionality, infrastructure engineers build the platforms, services, tooling, and systems that support those products.

Infrastructure often becomes more important as organizations grow.

---

# Core Areas

## Distributed Systems

Distributed systems consist of multiple computers working together to solve problems.

Common challenges:

* Scalability
* Reliability
* Availability
* Fault Tolerance
* Consistency

Examples:

* Kafka
* Cassandra
* Kubernetes
* CockroachDB

## Observability

Observability helps teams understand how systems behave.

Three Pillars:

### Logs

Records of events.

### Metrics

Numerical measurements.

### Traces

Request tracking across systems.

Common Technologies:

* Prometheus
* Grafana
* OpenTelemetry
* Datadog

---

Common Concepts:

* Uptime
* SLAs
* SLOs
* Error Budgets
