# Engineering Ecosystems

This page maps technical ecosystems and the relationships between companies, universities, open-source communities, research labs, internships, and engineering talent.

Rather than looking at companies individually, I find it more useful to understand the broader ecosystems that help develop technical talent.

## What Is An Engineering Ecosystem?

An engineering ecosystem consists of:

* Companies
* Universities
* Research Labs
* Open Source Communities
* Conferences
* Internships
* Fellowships
* Meetups
* Professional Networks

Together, these communities help shape how engineers learn, grow, and develop throughout their careers.
