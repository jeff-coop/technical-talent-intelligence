# US Engineering Talent Landscape

This page explores major engineering talent hubs across the United States.

The goal is to better understand where technical talent develops, which universities and companies influence local ecosystems, and how engineering communities evolve over time.

Rather than focusing on individual companies, this page looks at broader talent markets.

# Observations

Some themes I continue to notice:

* Infrastructure talent is heavily concentrated in Seattle, the Bay Area, New York, and Austin.
* Quantitative engineering talent is concentrated in New York and Chicago.
* AI talent is concentrated in the Bay Area, Boston, New York, and Seattle.
* Research-heavy talent is often connected to top universities and research labs.
* Open-source communities frequently overlap with strong engineering ecosystems.
