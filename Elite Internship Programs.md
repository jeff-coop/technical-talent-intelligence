# Elite Internship Programs

This page tracks internship programs that consistently attract, develop, and retain exceptional technical talent.

The goal is not to rank companies by prestige.

Instead, I'm interested in understanding which programs provide strong mentorship, meaningful ownership, technical depth, and exposure to complex engineering challenges.

Many future staff engineers, founders, researchers, and technical leaders begin their careers through these programs.

# What I Look For Beyond The Internship

The internship itself is only one signal.

I generally care more about:

* Ownership
* Technical depth
* Curiosity
* Learning velocity
* Open source contributions
* Research
* Leadership
* Communication

The experience and impact often matter more than the company logo.
