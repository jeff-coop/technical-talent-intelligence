# Infrastructure Engineering

## What Is Infrastructure Engineering?

Infrastructure engineering focuses on building and maintaining the systems, platforms, tools, and services that help software teams build, deploy, scale, and operate products reliably.

While product engineers often build customer-facing features, infrastructure engineers build the systems underneath those products.
