# Conferences, Competitions & Communities

This page tracks conferences, competitions, technical communities, hackathons, and organizations that consistently attract strong engineers, researchers, founders, and technical leaders.

Many of the best engineers spend significant time learning, competing, teaching, contributing, and networking outside of their day-to-day jobs.

Understanding these ecosystems can provide valuable insight into how technical talent develops.
